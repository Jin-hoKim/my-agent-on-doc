Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Docklings ("we", "our", or "the app") is a macOS desktop companion application developed by Jin-ho Kim. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use Docklings.

1. Information We Collect

Docklings collects and processes the following data:

  • Chat Messages: Text messages you type into the app to converse with the AI companion. These messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses.
  • API Key: If you use API mode, your Anthropic API key is stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Chat messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API solely to provide AI-powered conversation functionality.
  • We do not store your chat messages on any server. All conversation data is processed in real-time and stored only on your local device.
  • We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or tracking purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Docklings uses the following third-party service:

  • Anthropic Claude API: Your chat messages are sent to Anthropic's servers to generate AI responses. Anthropic's use of this data is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

  • All app data (chat history, settings, API keys) is stored locally on your Mac.
  • No data is stored on our servers — we do not operate any servers.
  • You can delete all app data at any time by removing the app and its associated data from your system.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties, except as described in Section 3 (sending chat messages to Anthropic's API for processing).

6. Children's Privacy

Docklings is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
